# AI-Native Product Creation

## The Problem

Creating a product on Shopify is painful. Merchants face a wall of fields, tabs, and options:
- Title, description, tags, categories, SEO fields, variants, pricing, inventory, shipping weight…
- Product images require separate photoshoots or freelance designers
- Descriptions require copywriting skills or hiring a writer
- SEO metadata is an afterthought most merchants skip entirely
- Categorization and tagging is manual busywork
- The whole process takes 30–60 minutes per product, even for experienced merchants

For new merchants, this is overwhelming. For scaling merchants, it's a bottleneck. Either way, Shopify gives you a blank form and says "figure it out."

## The Amboras Approach

Product creation is AI-native from the start. The merchant provides the minimum — a name, a rough idea, maybe a photo of the physical product — and AI handles the rest.

### What AI generates:
- **Product images** — Professional-quality product photos from a simple input (a phone photo, a text description, or a reference image). Studio-lit, white background, lifestyle shots, multiple angles. No photoshoot needed.
- **Product descriptions** — Conversion-optimized copy that matches the store's brand voice. Short description, long description, bullet points — all generated.
- **SEO metadata** — Title tags, meta descriptions, URL slugs, alt text for images. Automatically optimized, not an afterthought.
- **Categories & tags** — Auto-classified based on the product type. No manual tagging.
- **Variants** — Suggest logical variants (sizes, colors) based on product type and generate distinct images/descriptions per variant.
- **Pricing suggestions** — Based on product category, competitor analysis, and margin targets the merchant sets.

### How it works:
1. Merchant inputs the bare minimum (product name + a photo or description)
2. AI generates a complete product listing — images, copy, SEO, categories, everything
3. Merchant reviews, tweaks what they want, approves
4. Product is live

**Time to create a product: under 2 minutes vs. 30–60 minutes on Shopify.**

## Why This Is the Right First Feature

- It's the first thing every merchant does — create products. If this is 10x better, they feel the difference immediately.
- It directly validates the core thesis: Amboras doesn't just give you tools, it does the work.
- Product image generation alone is a massive pain point — merchants pay $50–200 per product for professional photos.
- It's a clear, demonstrable differentiator vs. Shopify in any pitch or demo.
- Every Shopify negative review about "too complicated" or "steep learning curve" traces back to this kind of complexity.

## Domain & Hosting Built In

Amboras handles domain purchasing and hosting as part of the platform — no third-party setup, no DNS headaches, no separate hosting provider.

- **Domain buying** — Search and purchase a custom domain directly inside Amboras. No need to go to GoDaddy or Namecheap and figure out DNS records.
- **Hosting included** — Every store is hosted by Amboras out of the box. No separate hosting plan, no server configuration, no uptime worries.
- **SSL & CDN** — Automatic HTTPS and global CDN. Merchant never thinks about it.
- **One less thing to figure out** — New merchants don't need to understand what hosting even means. They sign up, pick a domain, and their store is live.

This is table stakes for a platform like Shopify, but doing it well (fast, simple, zero friction) is part of the "we do the work for you" promise. Domain + hosting should feel like checking a box, not a project.
