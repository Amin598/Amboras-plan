## Key Patterns

### 1. Phone support eliminated, "24/7 Support" is false advertising
Shopify removed phone support entirely but still advertises "24/7 Support" on pricing pages. Merchants spend hours searching for a phone number that doesn't exist. Callback options are greyed out. This is the single most frequently cited complaint.

### 2. Chat support is painfully slow and ineffective
Agents handle multiple conversations simultaneously, leading to 30-45+ minute waits for simple questions. Sessions sometimes last 3+ hours. Agents occasionally end chats without resolving the issue.

### 3. AI chatbot gatekeeping replaces human support
Starting around 2023-2025, Shopify increasingly replaced human support with AI chatbots. Merchants report being stuck in chatbot loops for days or weeks. Human agents are hidden behind layers of bot deflection. Some merchants report you must pay more to reach a human.

### 4. Outsourced, untrained agents with no product knowledge
Multiple merchants report that agents lack basic understanding of how Shopify works. They give factually wrong answers (e.g., citing EU law for UK merchants), blame random third-party apps without investigating, and provide canned responses that don't address the actual problem.

### 5. Escalation is broken -- tickets closed without resolution
When merchants request escalation, support confirms it but then closes the ticket. No timelines are provided. No follow-up occurs. Formal complaints are acknowledged and then ignored.

### 6. Severe financial impact with zero urgency
Stores go down for 5+ days ($15k lost), funds are held for 120+ days, billing errors go unresolved for months. Shopify shows no urgency proportional to the financial damage merchants are experiencing.

### 7. Support quality decline is driving merchants away
Multiple merchants explicitly state they chose Shopify because of its support reputation. The degradation is severe enough that it has become the primary reason merchants leave the platform. This is a direct competitive vulnerability.

---

## Relevance to Amboras

This is a massive opening. Shopify's support was once a competitive advantage -- merchants chose it specifically for the support. Now it's a liability. Amboras can exploit this by:

- **AI-native support that actually works**: Unlike Shopify's chatbot that deflects, Amboras AI should genuinely understand the platform and resolve issues. The bar is on the floor.
- **Proactive problem detection**: Instead of waiting for merchants to discover their store is down, detect and fix issues automatically.
- **No hidden support tiers**: Transparent, accessible support for all plan levels. No greyed-out buttons, no bot gatekeeping.
- **Fast financial issue resolution**: Payment holds and billing errors need same-day resolution, not 120-day black holes.
- **Direct human escalation path**: When AI can't solve it, a clear, immediate path to a human who has context on the issue.
