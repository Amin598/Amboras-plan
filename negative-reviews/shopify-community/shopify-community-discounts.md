## Key Patterns

### 1. Discount stacking is the #1 pain point
Shopify's "combining" feature is misleading — it picks the largest discount and drops the rest, not true stacking. This has been requested since at least 2017 and remains unsolved. Merchants lose sales to cart abandonment when customers can't apply multiple valid promotions.

### 2. Discounts break silently and unpredictably
Codes stop working mid-sale with no warning. Checkout breaks on mobile. Shopify platform updates silently break existing discount configurations (e.g., the summer 2022 stacking update). Merchants discover the problem only when customers complain or sales drop.

### 3. Hard caps force merchants to third-party apps
25 automatic discounts max. 100 products per discount code. No variant-level targeting. These arbitrary limits push merchants to paid apps for what should be basic native functionality — tiered pricing, bulk discounts, large-catalog promotions.

### 4. No granular control over discount logic
Cannot specify which discounts stack with which. Cannot reliably target specific customer segments (guest checkout bypasses restrictions). Cannot limit per-customer usage beyond "1 use." Cannot apply BOGO to multiples in a single cart. The discount engine is too rigid for real-world promotional strategies.

### 5. BOGO and Buy X Get Y are unreliable
Invalid code errors, discounts applying only to the first pair, incompatibility with subscriptions, and tax calculation mismatches. Most merchants end up needing a third-party app for even basic BOGO promotions.

### 6. POS discounts are broken and acknowledged as such
Shopify's own support has admitted POS discount codes haven't worked properly "for a long time." Tickets go unanswered. This is particularly damaging for omnichannel merchants who need unified promotions across online and in-store.

### 7. Support cannot resolve discount issues
Multiple merchants report that Shopify support is unable to diagnose or fix discount bugs, with tickets going days without resolution or being abandoned entirely. Merchants are left to find workarounds themselves or accept lost sales.

---

**Amboras opportunity:** A discount and promotions engine that supports true stacking, granular combination rules, unlimited automatic discounts, customer-segment targeting without leaky codes, reliable BOGO/tiered pricing, and unified online + POS behavior — all native, no apps required.
