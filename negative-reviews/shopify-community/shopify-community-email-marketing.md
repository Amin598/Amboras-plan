## Key Patterns for Amboras

### 1. Deliverability is broken at a fundamental level
Emails going to spam even with perfect authentication (DMARC, DKIM, SPF all set up), and Shopify support cannot fix it. This is the single most common complaint category. Merchants are paying for an email service that literally does not deliver their emails.

### 2. Silent automation failures with no recovery
Automations stop working with zero notification. Merchants only discover the problem when they manually check or when customers complain. There is no health monitoring, no status alerts, no failure dashboard. When an email fails, there is no way to resend it individually. Failed = lost forever.

### 3. Template system is a dead end
No custom HTML import, no template reuse across campaigns and automations, no external design tool integration. The campaign editor and the automation editor are siloed — templates created in one cannot be used in the other. Merchants who migrate from Klaviyo discover they cannot bring their templates.

### 4. Segmentation is primitive and unreliable
No engagement-based filtering (opens, clicks, site visits). Segments break silently — customers disappear from segments overnight without explanation. Filters produce incorrect results. Merchants cannot even identify which customers were excluded from a send. Compared unfavorably to Klaviyo and Mailchimp by multiple users.

### 5. Analytics are unreliable and merchants don't trust them
Sales attribution is wrong (sometimes off by 4x). Open rates fluctuate without explanation. Entire analytics dashboards report zero despite confirmed engagement. Merchants cannot make data-driven decisions because the data is wrong.

### 6. Support is non-existent for email issues
Multiple merchants report support opening tickets and then never following up. During Black Friday, the most critical sales period, a merchant's emails stopped delivering and support ghosted them. The pattern is consistent: support acknowledges the issue exists but provides no fix.

## Amboras Opportunity

Every one of these problems is solvable with an AI-native approach:

- **Deliverability**: Built-in domain authentication wizard, real-time deliverability monitoring, proactive alerts before emails hit spam
- **Automation reliability**: Health dashboards, automatic failure detection and retry, one-click manual resend for failed emails
- **Templates**: AI generates email designs from a prompt, consistent design system across campaigns and automations, import from any source
- **Segmentation**: AI-powered segments based on behavior, engagement, purchase history — no manual filter building. Segments that explain themselves ("3 customers excluded because they unsubscribed yesterday")
- **Analytics**: Accurate attribution with transparent methodology, AI explains metric changes ("open rate dropped 15% because Gmail updated their proxy — your actual engagement is stable")
- **Support**: AI diagnoses email issues in real-time instead of waiting days for a human to open a ticket
