## Key Patterns for Amboras

### 1. Checkout is a Black Box
Merchants have zero control over checkout code. Shopify can change it at any time (one-page rollout, JS updates, shipping defaults) and merchants cannot opt out, revert, or customize. Even the Plus plan ($2K+/mo) offers only limited extensibility.

### 2. Forced Changes Destroy Revenue
The one-page checkout rollout caused 60-80% conversion drops for some merchants. Browser compatibility changes broke checkout entirely for customer segments. Shipping default changes caused order errors. Merchants have no say and no recourse.

### 3. Checkout Customization Requires Plus
Adding custom fields, surcharges, conditional logic, or custom payment methods requires the $2,000+/month Plus plan. Standard plan merchants cannot modify checkout at all. Even Plus merchants find the extensibility APIs "too limited."

### 4. Payment Provider Lock-in
Third-party payment providers face 2% penalties, broken integrations (PayPal Express), misleading currency warnings, and sudden removal of payment methods. Shopify pushes merchants toward Shopify Payments, then freezes funds without explanation.

### 5. No Graceful Degradation
When checkout breaks (buttons not working, payment methods disappearing, Apple Pay skipping pages), merchants lose sales with no fallback. There is no "safe mode" or degraded checkout experience.

### Amboras Angles
- **Merchant-controlled checkout:** Full checkout customization without enterprise pricing
- **No forced updates:** Merchants approve checkout changes before they go live
- **Checkout extensibility for all plans:** Custom fields, surcharges, conditional logic, delivery dates
- **Payment provider freedom:** No penalties for third-party providers, no fund freezes without due process
- **Resilient checkout:** Graceful fallbacks when components fail, never a blank page
- **AI-powered checkout optimization:** Automatically test and suggest checkout improvements based on conversion data
