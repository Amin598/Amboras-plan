## Key Patterns

1. **Support collapse**: The single most common complaint. AI chatbots replaced humans. No phone support. Scripted responses. Weeks-long resolution times for simple issues.
2. **Funds held hostage**: Multiple merchants report payouts frozen for 4-6+ months with no explanation, no escalation path, and scripted non-answers.
3. **Website builder is archaic**: Requires HTML/CSS knowledge for basic tasks. Other platforms (Wix, Squarespace) do drag-and-drop better.
4. **Hidden fees and bait-and-switch pricing**: Advertised rates don't include all fees. Features progressively moved behind paywalls. Essential tools cost extra.
5. **Platform instability**: Analytics breaking, saves failing, logouts losing work, POS systems crashing during peak periods.
6. **Product/storage limits despite "unlimited" marketing**: Hard caps on product uploads, storage, variants. Support admits platform doesn't support large catalogs.
7. **Former advocates turning critics**: Long-term merchants (6-11+ years) saying they would no longer recommend Shopify.
8. **Business-killing bugs with no emergency support**: Critical issues (customers can't log in, analytics dead, payments broken) with no way to reach anyone urgently.
