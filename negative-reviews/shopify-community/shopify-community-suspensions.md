# Shopify Community Forum — Account Suspensions, Bans & Store Closures

Source: community.shopify.com (official Shopify Community forums)
Collected: March 2026

---

## Summary

Account suspensions, bans, and store closures without warning or explanation are a massive recurring theme on the Shopify Community forums. Merchants report:

- Stores terminated with no prior warning and no explanation
- Generic AUP (Acceptable Use Policy) violation emails with no specifics
- Accounts closed before the merchant even added products
- Funds frozen for weeks or months after termination
- Support providing scripted non-answers and endless "escalation" loops
- No functioning appeal process
- Loss of all store data, themes, and customer relationships
- Financial damages in the thousands of dollars

## Key Patterns for Amboras

### 1. Automated Termination with No Human Review
Shopify appears to use automated systems that terminate accounts based on risk signals. There is no human review before termination, no warning, and no opportunity to provide information. Multiple merchants were terminated before they even had products.

### 2. No Due Process
Merchants have no functioning appeal mechanism. The appeal email is "not monitored." Support agents cannot help. The Risk Operations team takes 2-4 weeks to respond with a short, generic email.

### 3. Funds Held Hostage
After termination, pending payouts are held for 120 days. Merchants lose access to their money with no timeline and no recourse.

### 4. Total Data Loss
When terminated, merchants lose access to their admin, their themes, their product data, their customer lists, and their order history. Years of work gone instantly.

### 5. Disproportionate Response
A single chargeback can shut down a store for 4+ months. A billing info update can trigger suspension. The platform treats every risk signal as a nuclear event.

### 6. Financial Devastation
Merchants report $10K+ in losses from suspensions. Lost sales, lost customers, lost reputation, lost advertising spend, and the cost of rebuilding elsewhere.

---

## Amboras Positioning Angles

- **"Your store is yours."** Amboras will never close a store without warning, explanation, and a reasonable remediation window.
- **"Due process, not automation."** Human review before any account action. Clear communication about what's wrong and how to fix it.
- **"Your data, always."** Even in worst-case scenarios, merchants always retain access to export their data.
- **"Proportional response."** A chargeback triggers investigation, not instant shutdown. Verification requests come before suspension, not after.
- **"Your money, on time."** Funds are never frozen without clear legal obligation and transparent communication.
