## Key Patterns

1. **Data accuracy is fundamentally broken** -- Merchants cannot trust their own conversion rates, session counts, or checkout data. Multiple merchants report discrepancies of 50-98% between real orders and what Shopify analytics shows.

2. **Reporting updates removed features without replacement** -- Critical reports vanished overnight, 72-hour data delays were introduced, and basic queries now require SQL knowledge. Merchants describe business operations coming to a "complete standstill."

3. **Dashboard redesigns prioritized aesthetics over function** -- Hourly breakdowns, period comparisons, exact numbers, and multi-timeframe views were all removed. Even Shopify Plus merchants lost functionality.

4. **Cookie/GDPR handling destroys analytics silently** -- No clear guidance from Shopify. Merchants discover months later that their data has been incomplete. Shopify blames merchants for platform-level tracking failures.

5. **No fraud filtering in analytics** -- Canceled and refunded fraudulent orders permanently pollute dashboard metrics. No built-in way to see "real revenue" excluding fraud.

6. **Support is unhelpful** -- Multiple threads report Shopify closing tickets without resolution, blaming EU cookie laws for UK merchants, or simply saying "no issues found on our end."

### Amboras Opportunities

- **Real-time, accurate analytics by default** -- Server-side tracking that doesn't depend on cookies or client-side pixels. Orders placed = orders tracked. Always.
- **Merchant-first reporting** -- Reports designed around what merchants actually need (product performance, daily/hourly sales, fulfilled orders) not what looks good in a demo.
- **Fraud-aware dashboards** -- Automatic filtering of canceled/refunded/fraudulent orders from all metrics with toggle to include them.
- **No rounding, no hiding** -- Exact numbers visible at a glance. Multiple timeframes viewable simultaneously.
- **Instant data** -- Zero delay between a sale and it appearing in reports. Not 72 hours.
- **Plain-language custom reports** -- AI-powered report builder where merchants describe what they want in natural language instead of writing SQL.
