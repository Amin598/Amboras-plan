## Key Patterns for Amboras

### 1. Fee Stacking Creates Unpredictable True Cost
Merchants sign up at $39/month but the real cost is: subscription + transaction fees + tax calculation fees + payment processing fees + app fees + theme cost. Total can easily reach $500-1,000/month for a small store.

### 2. Fees Punish Growth
Tax fees are percentage-based, transaction fees scale with volume, and app pricing tiers up with usage. The more successful you are, the more you pay — with no corresponding increase in value.

### 3. Essential Features Gated Behind Expensive Plans
Real-time shipping ($399/month), advanced analytics, certain checkout customizations — features that directly impact conversion are locked behind plans most small merchants can't afford.

### 4. Third-Party Payment Penalty
The 2% fee for non-Shopify-Payments providers is an anti-competitive tax, especially punishing in countries where Shopify Payments isn't available.

### 5. No Fee Transparency
Merchants repeatedly discover fees after committing to the platform — during refunds, on specific payment methods, on tax calculations. The pricing page tells a partial story.

### 6. App Economy Is Broken
Apps that cost $5-10/month in 2020 now cost $200-600/month. Shopify benefits from this (they take a cut of app revenue) and has no incentive to build features natively.

### Amboras Positioning
- Transparent all-in pricing — one number, no surprises
- Essential features (shipping calculations, tax, reviews, SEO) included in every plan
- No penalty fees for payment provider choice
- No fees on refunded/cancelled transactions
- Fee structure that rewards growth instead of punishing it
