## Key Patterns

### Bulk Editor Is Fundamentally Broken
No multi-cell selection, loses product selections, white screens on load, forgets column preferences, auto-loads unwanted metafield columns that freeze the browser. Merchants call it "useless," "broken," and "a total bugfest." Updates consistently make it worse.

### 100-Variant Limit Blocks Entire Product Categories
Custom apparel, configurable goods, and large catalogs hit this wall constantly. Merchants report lost sales and excessive support emails from customers who cannot find the options they need.

### Product Descriptions Silently Fail to Save
Edits appear saved but revert on reload. Confirmed across browsers and incognito mode. A data-loss bug that persisted for 2+ years with no resolution.

### Metafields Are Powerful in Theory, Unusable in Practice
No reordering (3+ years unfixed, only workaround is delete-and-recreate). Confusing metaobject relationship. Values spontaneously corrupt across products. Merchants say they cannot "confidently use metafields in a sustainable and scalable way."

### Product Organization Is Primitive
Tags disappear randomly and break automated collections. No negation logic in collection rules. No real category hierarchy. Merchants describe organizing products as a "nightmare."

### Hidden Scale Limits
1,000 product/variant daily creation cap is undisclosed. Collection filters break above 5,000 products. CSV imports fail with server errors at scale. Shopify's answer is always "use a paid third-party app."
