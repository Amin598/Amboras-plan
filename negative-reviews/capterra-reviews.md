## Shopify Reviews

---

### Review 1

**Source:** [Shopify Reviews - Capterra](https://www.capterra.com/p/83891/Shopify/reviews/)
**Platform:** Capterra
**User:** Verified Reviewer — 11-year Shopify user, also used Shopify Plus
**Rating:** Low (exact score not visible)
**Date:** 2025

> **What they dislike:**
> After 11 years of use, overall very disappointed with Shopify. And don't be fooled that if you just upgrade, the offering will be better.
>
> I tried Shopify Plus and found it underwhelming to say the least, and a complete rip off on price. Part of the contract even includes not telling others how much it costs.
>
> Despite the price tag, their B2B offering didn't even have payment terms — no 30-day payment options. They couldn't even handle different billing and shipping addresses for wholesale orders.
>
> The support has become incredibly poor. You now have to convince an AI chatbot to let you speak to a human. Even paying a lot of money annually, you may not be able to speak to someone or expect a reply within 48 hours.
>
> Shopify appears to be constantly "updating" things, which actually seems to mean removing support or functionality. They repeatedly tell users "Shopify can't do that" or "can't do that anymore" so they need to use apps.

**Key issues:** 11-year user disappointed, Shopify Plus overpriced with NDA on pricing, B2B features missing, support degraded to AI chatbot, features removed via "updates"
