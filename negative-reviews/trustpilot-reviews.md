**Rating:** 1 star
**Title:** "Payment gateway barriers for Saudi and Middle East merchants"
**Reviewer:** Abdullateef
**Date:** 2026-03-07
**Text:** I started using Shopify to build an online store as an individual merchant in Saudi Arabia. I have a valid Freelance Work Document and a verified business bank account with Al Rajhi Bank, so I am fully eligible to run an online business. Unfortunately, the biggest obstacle I faced was the payment system integration.In many countries, Shopify Payments is available and merchants can start accepting payments almost immediately. However, Shopify Payments is not available in Saudi Arabia or most Middle Eastern countries. Because of this, merchants in the region are forced to rely on third-party payment gateways such as Moyasar, Tap Payments, or HyperPay.The problem is that activating these payment gateways is not simple at all. The approval process can take weeks, and the integration steps with Shopify are complicated and confusing for new merchants and freelancers. Many small business owners who are just starting do not have the technical experience required to handle these integrations.Because of this issue, my store received visitors but zero sales, not because the products were bad, but because customers could not complete the checkout process due to payment limitations. This is extremely frustrating for new entrepreneurs who are trying to start legitimate online businesses.Another major issue is the lack of strong Arabic language support. The Middle East is a rapidly growing market for e-commerce, tourism, digital services, and technology. Yet many parts of the Shopify platform still feel designed mainly for Western markets. Arabic merchants often face language barriers, payment barriers, and limited regional support.It would greatly help merchants in Saudi Arabia and the Middle East if Shopify improved its regional infrastructure by: Supporting more local payment gateways directly and simplifying the activation process, Providing stronger Arabic language support across the platform, Improving documentation and onboarding for Middle Eastern merchants, Expanding payment solutions similar to Shopify Payments in the region.Saudi Arabia and the Gulf region are investing heavily in digital transformation and e-commerce as part of national initiatives such as Saudi Vision 2030. Many freelancers and small entrepreneurs want to build online businesses, but payment integration challenges make it very difficult to get started.Shopify is a powerful platform with great potential, but without better support for payment gateways and regional needs in the Middle East, many independent merchants will struggle to use it effectively.
